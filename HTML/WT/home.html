ABES Engineering College is a private institute in Ghaziabad, affiliated with AKTU and approved by AICTE, known for its engineering programs in fields like Computer Science and IT. It offers B.Tech, MCA, and MBA courses, emphasizing industry-ready skills through Centers of Excellence and collaborations with companies like CISCO and EC-Council. With a focus on student development, the college provides a strong alumni network, ample internship opportunities, and a vibrant campus life. The college is recognized for its commitment to research, with faculty actively publishing and engaging in innovation. ABES features a well-equipped library and modern facilities, aiming to foster academic excellence and holistic student growth
